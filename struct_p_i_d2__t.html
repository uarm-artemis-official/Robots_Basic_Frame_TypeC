Detailed Description

Enhanced PID controller structure with additional parameters.

Implements a PID controller with beta and yeta parameters for setpoint weighting. Yeta may be used to reduce the effects of derivative kick. See this article for more details: https://jckantor.github.io/CBE30338/04.02-PID_Control_with_Setpoint_Weighting.html
